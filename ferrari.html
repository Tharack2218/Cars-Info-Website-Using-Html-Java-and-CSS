Ferrari cars in India

FERRARI

Ferrari car price starts at Rs 4.03 Crore for the cheapest model which is Portofino and the price of most expensive model, which is 812 starts at Rs 5.98 Crore. Ferrari offers 4 car models in India, including 1 car in Convertible category, 3 cars in Coupe category. Ferrari has 2 upcoming cars in India,....Purosangue SUV and Portofino Facelift.

Italian marquee Ferrari was founded back in 1939 by Enzo Ferrari. The company has its Headquarters in Maranello, Italy. Ferrari features the famous prancing horse logo finished in a shade of black with a yellow background on every model.

Ferrari has two dealers in India, located in Mumbai and New Delhi. The company has a wide range of products or cars to choose from, including the 812 Superfast, 812 GTS, SF90 Stradale, F8 Tributo, F8 Spider, 488 Pista, 488 Pista Spyder, GTC4Lusso, GTC4LussoT, Portofino, Monza SP1 and Monza SP2.

Scuderia Ferrari is the motorsport division of the Italian brand through which it competes in a range of categories such as Formula 1. The company also undertakes special projects and oneff-off model orders tailor-made to suit the demands and taste of the customer. The car maker has not revealed any plans for electrification as yet.
